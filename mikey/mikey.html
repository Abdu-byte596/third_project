The Martial Arts Prodigy

Raised in his grandfather's dojo, young Manjiro was a natural fighting genius, effortlessly taking down older opponents with his signature nuclear kick. Even as a child, his unmatched strength laid the groundwork for a legend.

The Golden Era of Toman

Gathering his closest friends, Mikey founded the Tokyo Manji Gang to create a new era for delinquents. Leading from the front, the charismatic "Invincible Mikey" fought with absolute honor and loyalty.

The Underworld Kingpin

Severing all past ties to protect his friends, Mikey descended deep into the criminal underworld. As the supreme Don of Bonten, he ruled over Japan's worst syndicates with an empty, hollow gaze.

The Dark Impulsivity

Consumed entirely by tragedy and grief, Mikey's inner Dark Impulsivity took complete control. Stripped of his morals, he stands at the precipice of absolute destruction, moving forward as a tragic final force.
